Cancellation Policy

Our poolside suite has a separate entrance from the main house and can be accessed from the street without disturbing the residents of the house. The suite has one bedroom with a quee size bed and a private bathroom. We can provide a cot to squeeze in a third person. The suite opens to and overlooks our swimming pool (solar heated in summer), which is available to guests. Free street parking. Easy access to public transportation. Bicycles available for guest use.

We've added a coffee-maker and a microwave at the suggestion of previous guests, although the host will often provide hot coffee at 8 a.m.. and there is a coffee shop within easy walking distance.

If you are looking to stay more than a couple of days, you should be aware of the kitchen situation. The house has a kitchen, but we only allow you to bring in strictly vegetarian foods, and the house is only open when someone is home. (More often than not, but not always). On the other hand, we have a refrigerator/freezer for use in the garage attached to the suite, and we will provide a camping stove or gas powered bar-b-q to use by the pool We can also provide pots and pans, plastic plates and bowls, and plastic knives and forks.
